About

I am a passionate Software Developer with over 7 years of experience in developing web-based applications and cloud solutions. My expertise spans Python (Django/FastAPI), PHP (Laravel), JavaScript, TypeScript, React.js (Next.js), and Java (Spring Boot, Servlet), along with MySQL and PostgreSQL. Currently, I am working at Deloitte Tohmatsu Node LLC (D.Node), where I specialize in Generative AI-based projects and cloud-native applications.
